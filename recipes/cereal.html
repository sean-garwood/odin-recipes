Cereal

Description

This is another all-time classic: a bowl of cereal with some milk! I ate this basically every morning when I was a kid.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cereal
  • Ice-cold milk (whole milk recommended)

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into a bowl.
  2. Pour milk into the same bowl.
  3. Feel free to cut up some fruit or whatever and put it on top.
