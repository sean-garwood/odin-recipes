Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a classic recipe that mom used to make. I still enjoy it thoroughly to this day. A timeless classic!

Ingredients

  • Schmidt's Italian bread with sesame seeds
  • Welch's concord grape jelly
  • Great Value creamy natural peanut butter

Steps

  1. Put two pieces of bread on a plate, such that the bottoms of each piece are adjoined.
  2. Add a smear of peanut butter to each piece of bread. Distribute evenly.
  3. Add a spoonful of grape jelly to one of the pieces of bread, on top of the peanut butter smear.
  4. Join the two pieces of bread such that the jelly and peanut butter are sandwiched between each other.
  5. Enjoy with a cool glass of milk!
