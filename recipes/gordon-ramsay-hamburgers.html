Gordon Ramsay's Fucking Hamburgers

Description

This recipe is simple and rustic, ya? We take an American calssic--the hamburger--and baste it in the most fattening substances to add that extra burst of flavor.

This recipe is perfect for a long night of drinking with the lads, or the basic evening in with the wife. Here's how it's done:

Ingredients

  • 1 kg ground beef
  • 2 Tbsp dijon mustard
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • One stick of butter for basting

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients (save the butter) in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix the ingredients well.
  3. Form six balls of ground beef from the mixture.
  4. Melt and clarify the butter.
  5. Form patties from the balls of ground beef.
  6. Throw the patties into a frying pan over high heat. Cook until internal temp reaches 80 degrees Celsius.
  7. Baste the burgers in butter. Serve on a brioche bun, with horseradish, a leaf of romaine lettuce, and one thick slice each of fresh tomato and white onion.
